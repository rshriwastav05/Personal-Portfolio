About D.A.R.K

D.A.R.K (Digital Access to Resources & Knowledge) is an open, free‑to‑use e‑library designed for students, educators, and lifelong learners. Our mission is to make learning materials not only freely available, but also effortlessly discoverable.

With multilingual support for English, Nepali, and Hindi, we ensure inclusivity and reach. Accessibility is a cornerstone of D.A.R.K — from keyboard‑friendly navigation and high‑contrast design to descriptive alt‑text for all media.

Upload your PDFs, tag them with relevant topics, and empower learners to find what matters in seconds.
