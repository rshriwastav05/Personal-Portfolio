🧠 What Students Should Remember

🖥️ Classical

Checks possibilities step by step.

⚛️ Quantum

Uses superposition and interference to explore many possibilities more cleverly.

❗ Important

Quantum computers are NOT just faster calculators. They are better for some special problems.

🎓 Teacher's Explanation

If I ask you to solve:
20 ÷ 5 × 3 + 2 − 1

Both classical and quantum computers follow the same mathematical rules.

But if I ask you to find ONE correct answer hidden among thousands of possibilities, a quantum computer can search in a much smarter way.

🌟 Think of a quantum computer not as "doing all calculations at once," but as "exploring many possibilities and making the correct answer stand out."
